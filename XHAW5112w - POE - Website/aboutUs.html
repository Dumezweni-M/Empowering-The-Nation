About Us

Empowering the Nation is a non-profit organization that provides skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Our mission is to uplift domestic workers and gardeners by making them more marketable when seeking employment and enabling them to be paid at a higher rate because of their additional skills. We also offer courses for employers who would like to upskill their employees to offer more advanced services to the household.
